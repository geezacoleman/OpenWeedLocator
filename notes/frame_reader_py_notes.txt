################################################################################

Purpose:
- Provide a unified interface for reading frames from images, videos or directories
- Used for testing algorithms without a live camera

Class:
- FrameReader: loads media, iterates over frames, and supports looping

Key Methods:
- read: returns next frame depending on input type
- reset: restart iteration
- stop: cleanup video stream

Dependencies:
- cv2
- imutils FileVideoStream
- utils.log_manager
