Purpose:
- Neural-network based weed detector supporting multiple accelerators
- Uses YOLOv5 models with Hailo, Coral Edge-TPU or CPU back ends

Class:
- GreenOnGreen: manages model loading and inference

Key Methods:
- inference: dispatch to hardware-specific implementation
- fps: return instantaneous frames per second

Dependencies:
- numpy, cv2
- optional: hailo_platform, pycoral, tflite_runtime
