Purpose:
- Save image frames or cropped regions to disk asynchronously

Class:
- ImageRecorder: manages worker processes and queue of frames

Key Methods:
- add_frame: queue new frame for saving
- save_images: worker loop processing queued frames
- stop and terminate: shutdown helpers

Dependencies:
- cv2, numpy
- multiprocessing, datetime
- utils.log_manager
